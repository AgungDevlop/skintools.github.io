Privacy Policy for Neon Core Engine

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

Agung Dev Team ("we," "us," or "our") develops and operates the Neon Core Engine mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of user privacy and transparency. This Privacy Policy document explicitly outlines our operational practices to ensure full compliance with Google Play Developer Program Policies.

1. Zero Personal Data Collection

Neon Core Engine does NOT collect, store, track, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII), device identifiers, or usage data. The App is designed as a strict, locally-executing hardware resource management engine. No user data is transmitted to external servers.

2. App Monetization and Third-Party Services

To provide an uninterrupted and secure user experience, we explicitly declare the following:

  • No Advertisements: The App does not contain, integrate, or display any third-party advertisements or ad networks (e.g., AdMob, Unity Ads).
  • No In-App Purchases (IAP): The App does not offer any in-app purchases, subscriptions, or hidden microtransactions.
  • No Analytics Tracking: We do not embed any third-party behavioral tracking SDKs or remote analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics) that could compromise device privacy.

3. Nature of the Application

To prevent misclassification, we clarify the functional scope of our App:

  • System Utility Engine: Neon Core Engine is designed exclusively to manage local system resources (such as CPU rendering priorities and memory allocation) to provide comfort and stability during mobile gaming sessions.
  • Not a Web Browser: This App does not contain web browsing capabilities, WebView interfaces for internet surfing, or functionalities to intercept network traffic and search queries.

4. Required Local Permissions

The App may require specific local system permissions to execute advanced background performance commands. These permissions are strictly utilized to communicate with the Android system architecture to deliver the advertised optimizations. At no point are these permissions used to access your personal files, media, contacts, location, or communications logs.

5. Children's Privacy

Since our App does not collect any data, it inherently protects the privacy of all users, including children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from anyone.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any modifications will be posted directly on this page with an updated "Effective Date" at the top of the document.
